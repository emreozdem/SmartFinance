Emre ÖZDEM - Founder of SmartFinance

About Us

The company quickly gained attention through technological advancements and smart banking principles, attracting a growing customer base. Today, SmartFinance hosts three branches and serves over 5 million customers. The company plans to expand globally by opening its fourth branch in New York City, aiming to become a global financial leader.

Founding Story

SmartFinance was founded in 2005, when Emre Özdem, a graduate of Çukurova University in Turkey, developed a banking application as his graduation thesis project. After graduating, Emre further developed the project and established it as a startup company. With successful investments, the company reached its first customers.
